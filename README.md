## Overview
Multi-Agent AI for Legal Intelligence is a smart, AI-driven platform designed to simplify and streamline the analysis of judicial cases. The system integrates multiple intelligent modules that work collaboratively to assist legal professionals in processing legal documents, extracting insights, and automating petition drafting.

The platform leverages Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Conversational AI to improve efficiency, accuracy, and decision-making in legal workflows.

---

## Core Modules

### 1. Petition Pipeline
- Acts as the primary processing unit.
- Ingests raw legal documents and performs preprocessing.
- Applies ML models to:
  - Classify case data
  - Summarize court cases
- Produces structured outputs ready for legal drafting and analysis.

---

### 2. ACT Module
- Performs advanced legal analysis on processed data.
- Automatically:
  - Tags relevant legal clauses
  - Extracts key legal features
  - Generates detailed summaries
- Helps identify important patterns and correlations in case data.

---

### 3. Law Bot
- A conversational AI assistant for legal interaction.
- Enables users to:
  - Ask legal queries
  - Get case insights
  - Generate draft petitions
- Uses outputs from the Petition Pipeline and ACT Module for intelligent responses.
